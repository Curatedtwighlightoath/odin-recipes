Chicken alfredo is one of the healthiest to both you physically and financially. Chicken is cheaper than most meats, and pasta and alfredo sauce are as well.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • alfredo sauce
  • basil
  • parmesan cheese

Recipe Steps

  1. Dice chicken into 1 inch chunks, while bringing water to a boil.
  2. Once water is boiling, put pasta into water and salt.
  3. Begin pan frying or grilling chicken. Once close to being done with the chicken, being bringing the alfredo sauce to a simmer in a sauce pan.
  4. zOnce all is done cooking combine and add the parmesan and basil.
