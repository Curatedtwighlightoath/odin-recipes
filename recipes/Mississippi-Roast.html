Mississippi Roast Recipe

The Mississippi Roast is one of the greatest dishes one can make, and it is surprisingly easy to do. It is a slow cooking process that is almost entirely hands off after the Roast is in the crockpot; and to make it even better, the recipe only calls for 5 ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Chuck Roast
  • Ranch dressing mix
  • Au jus gravy mix
  • Butter
  • Jarred pepperoncini peppers

Recipe Steps

  1. Place chuck roast in the bottom of the crockpot.
  2. Sprinkle the ranch and au jus mixes over the roast.
  3. Add the butter and pepperoncini peppers.
  4. Place the lid on top and cook over low heat for 8 hours or more.
